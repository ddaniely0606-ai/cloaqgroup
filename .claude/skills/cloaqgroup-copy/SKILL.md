## Voice & Tone

**What CloaqGroup sounds like:**
- Confident, never arrogant
- Direct — no filler, no fluff
- Premium — every sentence earns its place
- Israeli directness meets global agency polish
- Short punchy sentences. Occasional fragments. For impact.
- Data-backed claims over empty promises

**What CloaqGroup does NOT sound like:**
- Corporate jargon ("synergizing", "leveraging", "best-in-class")
- Overclaiming without proof
- Generic agency speak ("we're passionate about your success")
- Soft or apologetic
- Chatty or casual

**Tone by context:**
- Headlines: bold, short, provocative — 3–7 words max
- Body: authoritative but human, specific over vague
- CTAs: action-first, no "click here" — use "בואו נדבר", "תתחילו עכשיו", "הגיע הזמן"
- Stats/results: always lead with the number, then context

## Page-Type Templates

### Hero / Landing Page Headline
Pattern: `[מה אנחנו עושים] + [למי] + [תוצאה]`
Example: "אנחנו הופכים מותגים ישראלים לכוח שהתחרות מפחדת ממנו."

### Service Page Structure
1. Headline (provocative, 5–7 words)
2. Sub-headline (what it is + why it matters)
3. 3 bullet proof-points with numbers
4. CTA: "בואו נדבר על המותג שלך"

### Case Study Structure
1. Client name + category
2. Challenge (1 sentence)
3. What we did (2–3 bullets)
4. Result (BIG number first, bold)

### Ad Copy (Facebook/Instagram)
- Hook (first line stops the scroll): question or bold claim
- Problem agitation (1 sentence)
- Solution + proof (1–2 sentences)
- CTA (direct, urgent)
- Hebrew only, mobile-first, max 150 chars for hook

### Email Subject Lines
- Hebrew, 6–9 words
- Use numbers: "5 סיבות שהמותג שלך לא גדל"
- Use fear/curiosity: "המתחרים שלך כבר יודעים את זה"

## What to avoid in ALL copy

- "אנחנו מאמינים ב..." — weak
- "הפתרון המושלם עבורכם" — generic
- "צוות מקצועי ומנוסה" — every agency says this
- Passive voice where active is possible
- More than 2 exclamation marks per page
- English filler words mid-Hebrew sentence (just pick one language per sentence)
